What This Is
Trail runners, organized as stewards.
Trail Kind is a membership community that activates trail runners to maintain trails, support land managers, protect access, and build community around the places we love to run.
We believe that the people who benefit from trails should have easy, meaningful ways to take care of them. Not through guilt — through pride, belonging, and showing up.
"In ten years, the norm is: of course I spend a few days a year working on trails."

What We Believe
Trails are a public good.

Access should not depend on wealth or user fees. The people who benefit from trails should have opportunities to maintain and protect them. That's what Trail Kind is for.

Stewardship — we leave trails better than we found them
Service — we give back
Community — trails connect people as much as places
Partnership — we work with land managers
Access — everyone deserves access to trails
